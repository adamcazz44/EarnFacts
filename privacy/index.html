Privacy policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

The short version: EarnFacts is a simple content site and doesn't collect personal information from you directly.

What I collect

EarnFacts has no accounts, no sign-up forms, and no comment system. I don't ask for or store your name, email, or any personal details.

Hosting

The site is served as static pages. Like almost all websites, the host may record standard technical request logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. I don't control or use those logs for tracking.

Affiliate links

Some outbound links are affiliate links. If you click one, the destination site or its affiliate network may set its own cookies to credit the referral. That happens on their side, under their privacy policies, not mine. See the affiliate disclosure for details.

Analytics and ads

I don't currently run analytics or display ads. If that changes, I'll update this page to explain what's used and what it collects before turning it on.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it.
